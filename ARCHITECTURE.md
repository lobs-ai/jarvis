# jarvis

An always-on voice presence: talk to it (or type), it answers aloud and shows things on a stage
in sync with its voice, acts on the browser, and maintains a personal wiki about Rafe.

**The design of record is [docs/design/jarvis.md](docs/design/jarvis.md).** An earlier sketch
that lived in this file proposed squad as the brain and a five-plane framing; both were revised
during design review — see the Alternatives section of the design doc. Nothing has been
implemented yet.
